About Climate Africa SOS (U) Ltd

Our journey, mission, and commitment to environmental sustainability in Uganda

Our Background

Climate Africa SOS (U) Ltd was established in response to the growing environmental challenges facing communities in Kanungu District and across Uganda. Founded by local environmental advocates and community leaders, our organization emerged from a deep understanding of the interconnected relationship between environmental degradation, climate change, and community livelihoods.

Located in the heart of Kanungu District, an area rich in biodiversity but vulnerable to climate impacts, we recognized the urgent need for coordinated action to protect our natural resources while empowering local communities to build sustainable futures. Our establishment was driven by the belief that effective environmental conservation must go hand-in-hand with community development and economic empowerment.

Since our inception, we have grown from a small community initiative to a recognized non-profit organization making significant contributions to climate action, wetland restoration, forest conservation, and sustainable development in our region and beyond.

Our Core Objectives

💧

Wetland Restoration

Restore and protect critical wetland ecosystems through sustainable management practices, community engagement, and biodiversity conservation initiatives.

🌳

Tree Planting & Reforestation

Combat deforestation through large-scale tree planting initiatives, avocado seedling distribution, and community forest management programs.

🔥

Renewable Energy Promotion

Promote clean energy solutions and fuel-efficient technologies to reduce carbon emissions and improve community health outcomes.

🎓

Skills Development

Build capacity among youth and women through entrepreneurship training, vocational skills development, and sustainable livelihood programs.
