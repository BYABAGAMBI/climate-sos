Projects & Impact

Measuring our success and celebrating achievements in environmental conservation and community development

Transforming Lives and Landscapes

Since our establishment, Climate Africa SOS (U) Ltd has made significant strides in environmental conservation and community empowerment across Kanungu District. Our impact is measured not just in numbers, but in the lasting positive changes we see in communities and ecosystems.

Through dedicated implementation of our programs, we have successfully restored degraded environments, created sustainable livelihoods, and built community resilience to climate change impacts.

Success Stories

👩‍🌾

From Unemployment to Entrepreneurship

Sarah's Journey: After participating in our bakery training program, Sarah started her own successful bakery business, now employing 5 other women from her community. Her monthly income increased from $50 to $300, enabling her to support her family and invest in her children's education.

5 Jobs Created 6x Income Increase
🐝

Beekeeping Success Story

Kyagulanyi Youth Group: This group of 15 young people received beekeeping training and equipment. They now manage 50 beehives, producing over 500kg of honey annually. Their success has inspired other youth groups to adopt beekeeping as a sustainable livelihood.

15 Youth Empowered 500kg Honey/Year
🌲

Community Forest Restoration

Bushura Village: Through our tree planting initiative, Bushura Village successfully restored 20 hectares of degraded land. The community now enjoys improved water availability, increased biodiversity, and sustainable fuelwood sources.

20 Hectares Restored 3,000 Trees Planted
