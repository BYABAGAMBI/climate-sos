Combating Climate Change, Empowering Communities

Climate Africa SOS (U) Ltd is a dedicated non-profit organization based in Kanungu District, Uganda, working at the forefront of environmental conservation and climate action. We believe that sustainable development and environmental protection go hand in hand with community empowerment.

Through our comprehensive programs, we address the interconnected challenges of climate change, environmental degradation, and economic inequality. Our approach combines practical conservation efforts with skills development and entrepreneurship training, creating lasting positive change for both people and planet.
