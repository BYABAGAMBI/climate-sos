How to Find Us

Getting to Our Office

Our office is conveniently located at Mt. Gorilla Inn in Bushura Village, Kirima Sub-County, Kanungu District. Here's how you can reach us:

🚗 By Private Vehicle

From Kampala: Take the Kampala-Masaka highway, then proceed through Mbarara to Kabale, and finally to Kanungu. Our office is well-signposted from the main town center.

🚌 Public Transport

Regular buses and taxis operate from Kampala to Kanungu town. From the town center, local taxis or boda-bodas can bring you to our office at Mt. Gorilla Inn.

✈️ By Air

The nearest airport is Entebbe International Airport. From there, you can take domestic flights to nearby airstrips or travel by road to Kanungu.

Local Information

  • Accommodation available at Mt. Gorilla Inn for visiting partners and volunteers
  • Parking facilities available on-site
  • Office accessible for persons with disabilities
  • WiFi available for visitors
